A little about me

Hi, hello, welcome to my program book I'm Alexandria Moreno but you can call me Alex. I'm a third year student at Arizona State University pursuing my Bachelors Degree in Graphic Design, and my graduation date is in May of 2023. My love for graphic design started in art, drawing, painting, and other crafts. Once I knew what graphic design entailed I knew that was my career path, creating visuals to communicate a meaningful message for an audience for a living. This is why in my spare time I enjoy making self published magazine known as a "zine". This project I purely for my enjoyment and it showcases art from local artist with information about them or if they choose to it can also be anonymous. As you can see my love for design is entangled in my life and I hope to continue that into my career life.
